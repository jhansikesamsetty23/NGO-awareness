About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving society through education, environmental protection, animal welfare, women empowerment and community development.

Ongoing Project - Project JEEV

Project JEEV focuses on protecting stray animals by providing food, water, medical care and creating awareness about kindness towards animals.

Social Impact

  • Encouraging environmental protection
  • Supporting community service
  • Building a caring society
